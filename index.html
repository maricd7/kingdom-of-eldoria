In the ancient Kingdom of Eldoria, peace and prosperity reigned for centuries under the wise rule of King Aric. However, darkness descended upon the land when the evil sorcerer Malakar appeared, seeking to plunge the kingdom into chaos. Using forbidden magic, Malakar captured the royal family and plunged Eldoria into an age of despair. You, a brave adventurer, must embark on a perilous journey to free the royal family and restore peace to Eldoria.
